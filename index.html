Game Instructions

5-5s.com/FlashCards is designed to be played using an online meeting application such as Zoom. After selecting 5 topics, the conductor shares their screen displaying the game board with 5 category columns each containing 5 FlashCards. When a FlashCard is selected, all players read the question and quickly try to be the first to type the correct answer in the Zoom chat window. The first correct reply wins the (5) points for that question for their team.

 

Players are grouped into teams based on their Zoom chat names. Names beginning with the letters A thru I are the Red team. Letters J thru Q are the Blue team, and the remaining players are the Green team.

 

When a question is selected, a (5 or 8) second timer begins. The first person to answer correctly wins the (5) points for their team. The conductor will need to add the (5) points to the winning players team after each question is answered correctly. Any player who submits an incorrect answer will be penalized by costing thier team one (1) point. No points or penalties will be attributed to players submitting a correct answer after the first correct answer is submitted.

 

If one team answers all 5 questions in a category, that team gets an extra 5 point bonus. The team with the highest score at the end of the game wins.
