Game Instructions

5-5s.com/FlashCards is designed to be played using an online meeting application such as Zoom. The meeting conductor shares their screen, selects 5 topics and clicks Start Game. One by one, each topic FlashCard is clicked and players have 5 seconds to type an answer in the chat. The first correct reply wins (5) points for their team. The team with the highest score at the end of the game wins.

Suggestions

Players can be grouped into teams based on their Zoom chat names.

  • Red team: Names beginning w/A thru I
  • Purple team: Letters J thru Q
  • Green team Letters R thur Z
 

Players who submit incorrect answers can cost thier team one (1) point.

Any team answering all 5 questions in a category, gets a 5 point bonus.
