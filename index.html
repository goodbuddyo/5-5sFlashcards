Game Instructions

FlashCards is designed to be played using an online meeting application such as Zoom. After selecting 5 topics, the conductor shares their screen displaying the 5-5s.com FlashCards game board with 5 columns each containing 5 FlashCards. When a FlashCard is selected, all players must read the question and quickly try to be the first to type the correct answer in the Zoom chat window wins the points for that question for their team.

 

Players are grouped into teams based on their Zoom chat names. Names beginning with the letters A thru I are on the red team. Those beginning with the letters J thru Q are on the blue team, and remaining players are on the third, green team.

 

When a question is selected, a 5 second timer begins. The first person to answer correctly wins 5 points for their team. The conductor, or a separate score keeper will attribute points to the winning players team. All players who submit an incorrect answer are penalized by thier team score being reduced by one point (so try to answer accurately). No points or penalties will be given to players submitting the correct answer after the first correct answer is submitted.

 

If one team answers all 5 questions in a category, that team gets an extra 5 point bonus. The team with the highest score at the end of the game wins.
